Order Now!

Ordering from us is as easy as ordering your favourite pizza!
Get your artwork ready, and then:

1

Pick a size!

We can print and cut ANY size up to A3 (420mm x 297mm)!
If unsure, you can refer to these images (round and rectangular) to determine the sizes you want!
Example: If you want your sticker to be the size of a new $1 coin, you can tell us that you need 25mm stickers.

2

Pick a shape!

Just send us the artwork* and tell us what shape you want!
Example: If you want circular shape stickers for your homemade cookie business, just send us your artwork and let us know you need round stickers!

*Artwork must be in minimum 300dpi resolution, but preferably 400dpi.
Accepted file formats are: JPG, PNG, PSD, PDF, AI 

3

Decide how many stickers you want!

However many -or little! - you need, we can definitely do it! And yes, in 24 hours or less, guaranteed!
Want 10pcs to try? Sure, no problem.
Need a bigger batch of 500pcs? You got it!
Maybe you have 5 different flavours and need 100 each? Of course!

4

Choose a material!

We know it can be a little overwhelming with all the choices available, so you can also tell us what you need the stickers for and we can advise you further!
Pasting on a cookie bottle, or maybe a cake box? Mirrorkote it is!
Perhaps you are pasting on ice cream tubs or other icy treats? Synthetic will be perfect!

5

Optional: Select a cutting method!

All of our stickers are kiss-cut on sheets by default, so you will receive whole sheets of stickers that are easily peelable at once for pasting on your products!
However, if you need your stickers cut in another way, eg die-cutting to give out the stickers, just let us know!

With all that done, voila! You are ready to order your stickers!
