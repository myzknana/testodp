WHITE PVC STICKERS

Our strongest material yet.

White PVC stickers are made from Synthetic sticker material and then gloss laminated.  PVC is our toughest material yet - waterproof, weatherproof, oilproof, chemicalproof, tearproof... Yes, they can withstand nearly everything!

Gloss lamination also gives these stickers a beautiful shine, making them not only suitable for heavy duty usage on machinery and equipment, but also as gifts for any event, pasted on laptops and phones to help your customers and guests remember you.

*White PVC stickers can also come with removable adhesive. Please email to enquire.
*We also provide reverse printing if needed (ie printing on the sticky side). Please email to enquire.

Weatherproof

The lamination  on these stickers helps to protect these stickers against the harsh elements, be it sun or rain - you never have to worry when outdoors!

Waterproof, oilproof

Water and oils are repelled by the protective laminated surface, ensuring that they do not seep in and damage the actual sticker surface. This goes for essential oils too!

Scratchproof, Tearproof

Rest assured that frequent handling and scratches won't affect the print. Plus, they can't be torn either - perfect for heavy duty usage!

Chemical-proof

Lamination also ensures that these stickers remain chemical proof. Anything that might damage the print is repelled, ensuring their safety.

Writable

PVC stickers can be written on easily with oil-based markers, and you can be certain that they won't be easily wiped or rubbed off.

Premium Quality

PVC stickers may be more expensive than our standard stickers, but this extra cost also translates into high quality stickers that are guaranteed to last.

Usage Suggestions

Outdoors

If you need to label or decorate your sports equipment, sports bottles etc, look no further than PVC stickers! Again, being weatherproof, they are sure to withstand anything thrown at them - no worries of the stickers getting damaged even with frequent rough handling, or from exposure to the harsh elements!

Phones & Laptops

PVC stickers are perfect for pasting on personal belongings, most notably laptops and phones. Being carried and used every day of our lives, many love to decorate and add a personal touch to these items. With PVC stickers, rest assured that not only do your stickers look gorgeous, but will also last long! 

Essential Oil

Essential oil products, be it blends, balms or pure oils, are best served by PVC stickers. As essential oils are highly concentrated, they will actually damage unprotected prints. With PVC stickers, you no longer have to fear - the printed design remains perfectly safe and readable. You can be certain of your branding remaining safe and sound! 

Helmets

Being waterproof and weatherproof, PVC stickers are perfect either to decorate helmets, or to indicate company equipment and safety induction. You can be sure that they won’t drop or fade, whether under sun or rain, or when being washed - they will surely stay on perfectly!

EZ-Link

PVC stickers are often the most ideal for EZ-Link card stickers. Gloss lamination ensures that the printing isn't affected, despite friction from frequently handling the card. The strong adhesive also makes sure that your sticker stays securely on the card, no matter how much you use it!

Machinery

Your heavy duty machinery may need to be inspected and handled often, so be sure to get stickers that you know will last through frequent usage! Even if your machines and equipment are located outside, facing the harsh elements, or if there is a risk of exposure to oils and chemicals, you never need to worry with PVC stickers!

Extra Tips!

Being our thickest material, do note that they are less flexible than Synthetic stickers and curve less easily, making them unsuited for small items unless wrapped end to end.

PVC stickers are moderately heat resistant and can withstand short bursts of heat and weather. However, they cannot be used long-term on constantly heated objects (eg hot food display cases).

Be sure to paste PVC stickers on the dryest and most even surface possible, to help ensure that no bubbles appear on the surface due to its thickness!

Only markers can be used to write on PVC material stickers. Ballpoint pens cannot be used, and will not stay on the sticker surface.
