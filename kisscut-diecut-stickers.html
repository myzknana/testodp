STICKER CUTTING TECHNOLOGIES

  • Kiss-cut stickers
  • Custom sticker sets
  • Die-cut stickers

We print kiss-cut stickers by default. These stickers come in whole sheets and are easy to peel, making them most suitable for labelling many items at a time. They can come in any shape and size as well, be it round, square or otherwise - even custom shapes!

Our standard sticker sheets allow for only one shape and size to be printed on a sheet, for example:
✓ 1 design of 50mm round stickers
✓ 3 designs of 35mm square stickers
But not:
✗ 50mm round and 35mm square stickers together
✗ 50mm round and 35mm round stickers together

If you need stickers of various shapes & sizes together on a sheet, those can also be done - click on the Custom Sticker Sets tab to learn more! Or, if you need die-cut stickers to distribute, just click on the Die-cut stickers tab for more information!

We offer kiss-cut sticker sets, if you require stickers of multiple designs, shapes and sizes to be printed together on a sheet. The size of the sticker sheet can be customised to your needs as well.

Due to the customisation required of every set, do note that we will need to see, at the very least, a sketch of the intended artwork before we are able to give a quote. The sizes, shapes and quantities of the stickers needed on every sheet will affect the cost.

However, rest assured that the artwork provided need not be the final piece - we understand that changes may be made! Simply an approximation of the designs would be enough for a close estimation of the costs, and we will fine-tune the quotation should there be any changes later on.

Die-cut stickers come in separate pieces, with the sticker backing cut exactly to the sticker shape as well, be it round, square, etc. These stickers are perfect for sales and distribution, since they can be easily stacked up and handed out.

You can choose to have the stickers die-cut completely or individually cut from kiss-cut sheets, depending on your preference. When individually cut out, the backing will be in rectangles or squares. Otherwise, the backing will follow the shape of the sticker exactly for die-cut stickers.
