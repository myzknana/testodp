Frequently Asked Questions

When is the earliest I can get my stickers?

As promised, printing will be completed in 15 minutes to 24 hours after payment. Should you need them more urgently, please let us know and we will check for you! As every order is unique and our print jobs run on a queue, the exact timing is not often immediately guaranteed.

Is it possible to deliver my order at a specific time and date?

While the date can be chosen, please note that there are only two timeslots offered by our courier:
12pm to 6pm OR 6pm to 11pm

We can inform the courier of any requests, however we are still unable to guarantee that an order will reach at a specific time. If you urgently require an item delivered by a specific time, kindly top up for express delivery, where timings are more flexible. Otherwise, you can also book your own courier.

Please note that we do not offer any morning delivery timeslots, even with express delivery. The courier company we work with does not offer any timeslots in the morning.

Can I come down to your office?

Yes, but only if you have a walk-in appointment, which must be arranged via email. This allows us to first confirm that we are indeed able to print what is needed, and that artwork is suitable.

By arranging an appointment, we can also ensure that one of our sales representatives will be available to assist you at the desired time! 

Can my stickers be pasted on (surface)?

In order to have your stickers adhere securely, always ensure that the surface is dry & clean and free from dirt & dust. For curved objects, such as bottles and jars, ensure that the surface is a smooth curve (eg not tapered at one end).

Generally, smooth, non-porous textures are suitable for stickers. Foam, silicone, non-woven cloth etc. are not sticker-friendly surfaces. This is not to say that the stickers won't stick at all - they will, and do not drop off on their own. They will simply be easily peeled off due to the weaker adhesive hold.

Tapered and spherical objects are also unsuitable for stickers. Their curvature makes it such that stickers, when pasted, will form wrinkles and bubbles (more examples here)

If unsure, just drop us an email! Our helpful team is always happy to assist!

I don't have any software. Can you design something for me? 

We can, however there will be artwork fees of at least $40 depending on the complexity of the design.

You can look through the samples offered on the Download Samples page. There are also several methods of creating artwork listed, all of which are free and easy to use - many of our customers have used them before!

I don't have a soft copy, only a hard copy. Is that okay?

We cannot reproduce a high quality print unless we have the soft copy, or recreate it digitally. We can however print from a scanned copy, but the quality will not be as good as if it were from an actual soft copy. If brought to our office, scanning and touch-ups will cost $15. Otherwise, we can recreate the artwork at a fee of $40, minimum.

Can I get a sample before printing in bulk?

We do not print samples prior to mass printing. However, we will send you a digital copy of the final artwork for your approval, and highlight issues, if any. Do rest assured that printing only takes place after you have approved and confirmed the artwork!

What file formats are accepted?

We accept: AI, PDF, PSD, JPG and PNG, at a minimum resolution of 300dpi, with 400dpi preferred.

As for other file formats, eg DOC and PPT, please convert them to PDF first, which ensures that text is displayed accurately even if we do not have the font installed. 

Can you print before payment and I pay cash on collection?

No, we require upfront payment before any printing commences. If you are unable to make any payment beforehand (eg school requires e-Invoice), please let us know beforehand and we can make necessary arrangements. Cash payment on order is fine, but not on collection.

I've ordered before and want to reprint - how do I do so?

We store your order for up to 2 years. Simply drop us an email, preferably replying to the email thread of your previous order. After payment and confirmation, we will commence printing!

Why don't my printed colours look the same as my artwork on my computer or phone screen?

Colours on your screen are displayed using RGB light -making them appear much brighter, and able to recreate more colours than are available using ink and toner. While we will always try our best to make sure the colours printed are as accurate as possible, we suggest using generally darker shades, which are easier to reproduce in print, instead of neon colours.

It's very real - we do not have a minimum order at all! So yes, even a single sticker is perfectly fine to print.
