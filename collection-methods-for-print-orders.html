Delivery and Collection

Self collection

We will email you as soon as we have completed printing! Feel free to drop by anytime within our operating hours to collect your order!

You can also book your own courier, however do:
i) Instruct the courier to quote your name on collection and
ii) Let us know so we can pack your order in advance.

Monday to Saturday: 10.30am to 9.00pm
Closed on Sundays and public holidays

AM Mail

Next working day* urgent mail delivery service offered by SingPost. Click here to learn more!

Please note that for AM Mail, we are not liable for any lost mail, delays or damage.

$3.50 per 300g, inclusive of envelope.

*In our experience, mailing may still take 1-3 working days. Please select this option only if your order is not urgent.

*We do not offer normal mail, nor registered mail.

Courier delivery

Next-day* delivery right to your doorstep, the day after printing is completed.

Match the first 2 digits of your postal code for delivery rates.
01 to 10 : $12 | 56 to 59 : $12
11 to 13 : $15 | 60 to 73 : $15
14 to 33 : $12 | 75 to 80 : $12
34 to 55 : $10 | 81 to 82 : $10
Delivery to restricted areas eg Sentosa and Changi Airport will incur a $10 surcharge on top of the above.

*Mondays to Saturdays, 12-6pm or 6-10pm.

EXPRESS Courier Delivery

For super urgent orders, get your order delivered on the same day*!

$10 express surcharge on top of regular delivery rates.
Restricted area surcharge remains applicable.

*Within 3 hours after we complete your order. eg if printing is completed at 6pm, you can expect to receive your order by 9pm on the same day.
