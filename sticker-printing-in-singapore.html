Materials

Mirrorkote Material

Mirrorkote stickers are made of basic glossy paper and are the most cost-effective ones that we have. They are best for flat and smooth surfaces like box packaging and paper products.

Mirrorkote Stickers

Synthetic Material

Synthetic stickers are printed on thin, waterproof plastic material, best for curved and/or rough & uneven surfaces, such as bottles and beauty products.

Synthetic Stickers

White PVC Material

PVC stickers are synthetic waterproof plastic with gloss lamination. This is the most durable option that we offer. Best for electronics like laptops and phones, essential oil products, outdoor usage.

White PVC Stickers

Transparent PVC Material

Transparent PVC stickers are made of waterproof & weatherproof material with gloss lamination. Like regular white PVC stickers, they are highly durable and work well for electronics and equipment.

Transparent PVC Stickers

Metallic Foil PVC Material

Gold and silver foil PVC stickers are our most premium material. Not only do they look beautiful & luxurious, they are also ultra durable with gloss lamination. 

Gold Foil PVC Stickers Silver Foil PVC Stickers

Kraft Material

Kraft material stickers are matte paper with a natural brown finish. Like Mirrorkote stickers, they are best for smooth, dry and flat surfaces like boxes and paper products.

Kraft Stickers

Shapes

Round & Oval Stickers

Round stickers are, by far, our most common sticker shape. They are fantastic for events and product branding in particular. Oval stickers are also unique and perfect for wider logos, or for adding a little more information where needed!

Round & Oval Shape

Square & Rectangular Stickers

Square & rectangular stickers are classic shapes, best for information labels such as ingredient lists & nutrition facts. Rounded corners ensure durability & allow distribution.

Square & Rectangular Shape Rounded Corners Square & Rectangular Shape

Custom-Shaped Stickers

Custom-shaped stickers are perfect as gifts or promoting corporate events, especially with logos and mascot characters. They can even serve as unique, effective product branding - your custom-cut stickers will always be sure to stand out!

Custom Shape

EZ-Link Stickers

EZ-Link stickers are also perfect as gifts or to commemorate events, and are perhaps some of the simplest and most useful gifts possible - your guests and participants will surely remember your event fondly!

EZ-Link Stickers

Uncut Sticker Sheets

Have lots of designs but need only a few pieces each? DIY-cut sheets allow you to print as many as you need - just cut them out on your own! These are especially useful for fan merchandise or fundraisers!

Uncut sheets
